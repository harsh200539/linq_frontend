Job Overview

As a Market Research Analyst at LINQ Corporate Solutions, you will be at the forefront of delivering data-driven insights that shape strategic decisions for our global clientele. You'll work with cutting-edge research methodologies and collaborate with our international team of 350+ professionals to provide comprehensive market intelligence across 8 core industries.

This role offers an exceptional opportunity to gain exposure to diverse markets, work on high-impact projects, and contribute to our mission of delivering powerful insights that drive business success worldwide.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct comprehensive market research and analysis across multiple industries including Oil & Gas, Healthcare, Biotechnology, and Pharmaceuticals
  • Develop and maintain databases of market intelligence, competitor analysis, and industry trends
  • Create detailed reports and presentations that translate complex data into actionable business insights
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support strategic advisory projects
  • Monitor industry developments and emerging market opportunities across 41 countries
  • Utilize advanced analytical tools and methodologies to ensure data accuracy and relevance
  • Support client presentations and participate in strategic planning sessions
  • Maintain quality standards and ensure timely delivery of research deliverables
  • Contribute to the development of new research methodologies and best practices
  • Provide mentorship and guidance to junior research team members

Requirements & Qualifications

Required Qualifications
  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Economics, Statistics, or related field
  • 2-4 years of experience in market research or business analysis
  • Proficiency in data analysis tools (Excel, SPSS, R, or Python)
  • Strong analytical and critical thinking skills
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Experience with research methodologies and statistical analysis
Preferred Qualifications
  • Master's degree in relevant field
  • Experience in international market research
  • Knowledge of industry-specific trends and dynamics
  • Familiarity with business intelligence tools
  • Previous consulting or advisory experience
  • Multilingual capabilities (advantage)
